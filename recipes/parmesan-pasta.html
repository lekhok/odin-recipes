Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Description:

Indulge in the comforting embrace of Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta. This dish combines al dente fettuccine with a luscious, velvety sauce that marries the rich flavors of butter, heavy cream, and freshly grated Parmesan cheese. The journey begins by cooking the fettuccine to perfection. In a skillet, minced garlic sizzles in butter, releasing its aromatic essence. The stage is then set for the entrance of heavy cream, which swirls and mingles with the garlic, resulting in a luxurious base. The pièce de résistance arrives as Parmesan cheese is gently folded into the mixture, melting into a smooth symphony of flavor. A dash of salt and black pepper season the sauce, but balance is key, as the cheese already imparts its own salty notes. The cooked fettuccine pirouettes into the sauce, each strand luxuriating in its creamy embrace. A sprinkle of fresh parsley crowns the dish, adding a touch of color and herbal freshness. With each forkful, you'll savor the harmonious dance of textures and flavors, making Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta a timeless favorite.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (225g) fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and let it simmer gently for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Gradually stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until it's melted and the sauce is smooth.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Keep in mind that the Parmesan is salty, so be careful not to over-salt.
  6. Add the cooked fettuccine to the sauce and toss to coat the pasta evenly.
  7. Serve the creamy garlic Parmesan pasta in bowls, garnished with chopped fresh parsley.
