Roasted Vegetable Quinoa Bowl

Description:

Embark on a journey of wholesome nourishment with the Roasted Vegetable Quinoa Bowl. This recipe celebrates the harmonious union of nutty quinoa and an array of roasted vegetables. Quinoa takes center stage, prepared with care as it's cooked in vegetable broth, absorbing the savory essence for a delightful base. Meanwhile, a rainbow of vegetables dances on a baking sheet, their vibrant hues promising both beauty and flavor. Slices of red bell pepper, zucchini, yellow squash, red onion, and carrots are coated in olive oil and a medley of dried herbs, destined for the oven where they'll transform into caramelized gems. Once roasted to tender perfection, the vegetables are layered atop the quinoa like a work of art. Crumbled goat cheese, with its tangy and creamy character, adds a luxurious touch, while a drizzle of balsamic glaze brings a hint of sweetness and depth. Each bite encapsulates the essence of wholesome goodness, offering a satisfying blend of textures and flavors. The Roasted Vegetable Quinoa Bowl is more than a meal—it's a celebration of nature's bounty and culinary creativity.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups vegetable broth or water
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried herbs (such as thyme, rosemary, or oregano)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup crumbled goat cheese
  • Balsamic glaze for drizzling

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a saucepan, combine quinoa and vegetable broth (or water). Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat, cover, and simmer for about 15 minutes or until the quinoa is cooked and the liquid is absorbed. Fluff with a fork and set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, toss the sliced vegetables with olive oil, dried herbs, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Spread the seasoned vegetables on a baking sheet in a single layer. Roast in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes or until they are tender and slightly caramelized.
  5. To assemble the bowls, divide the cooked quinoa among serving bowls.
  6. Top with roasted vegetables, crumbled goat cheese, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze.
  7. Serve the roasted vegetable quinoa bowls as a delicious and nutritious meal.
